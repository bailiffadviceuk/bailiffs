Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you help?

We aim to respond to all enquiries within 24 hours. If your situation is urgent, please call our freephone number for immediate assistance.

Is your advice really free?

Yes, our initial advice is completely free. We'll explain all your options and if we recommend a paid solution like an IVA, we'll clearly explain all fees and implications before you proceed.

Can you help if I'm not in England?

Our advice is primarily for England and Wales, as bailiff laws differ in Scotland and Northern Ireland. Please contact us and we'll do our best to point you in the right direction.

What information should I have ready?

When you contact us, it's helpful to have details of your debts, any letters from bailiffs or the court, and information about your income and expenses.
